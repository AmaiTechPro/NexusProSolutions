Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Effective Date: December 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information to provide and improve our services to you. The types of information we collect include:

  • Identity Data: Full name, username, and email address (collected during registration).
  • Contact Data: Phone number (especially WhatsApp number for session scheduling and urgent support).
  • Professional Data:Service interest (e.g., CCNA training, career advisory), and professional bio (collected during profile editing).
  • Transaction Data: Details about services you have purchased or booked (e.g., booking reference IDs, payment status).
  • Technical/Usage Data:IP address, browser type, operating system, and data collected via cookies when you interact with our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is primarily used to ensure service delivery, provide support, and improve the client experience. Specific uses include:

  • Service Provision: To create and manage your account, verify identity, and provide access to purchased training materials and lab environments.
  • Communication: To schedule, confirm, or cancel booked sessions (often via WhatsApp/SMS using Contact Data).
  • Service Improvement: To monitor usage trends, diagnose technical problems, and optimize our course offerings and website features.
  • Marketing (with consent): To send occasional updates, newsletters, or promotional material regarding new courses or services. You can opt-out at any time.

3. How We Protect Your Data

We are committed to protecting your personal data. We implement reasonable security measures, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmission between your browser and our server is secured using SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Data Minimization:We only collect and retain data necessary for the provision of services.
  • Access Control:Access to PII is restricted to employees and contractors who need the information to perform their specific duties (e.g., technical support staff, career advisors).

4. Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personally Identifiable Information to third parties. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order).
  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting services) only to the extent necessary for them to perform their functions on our behalf.

5. Your Data Rights

Under Kenyan law and general data protection principles, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify: Request correction of any incomplete or inaccurate data we hold about you (manageable via the 'Edit Profile' page).
  • Object/Erase: Object to the processing of your data or request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention periods (e.g., for tax purposes).

6. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this policy periodically.

For questions or to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer:
